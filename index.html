Terms and Conditions

Once you are already a registered member of The Zenith Academy (TZC), there are certain rules that every individual must abide by and must agree to our terms and conditions;
       * Every individual must be at the tutorial center as early as 8:00am or latest 8:30am every working day of the week and also resume with the required notebooks and textbooks for the day.
       * Every individual must come with all past-questions relating to his/her department of study (Science, Commercial, Art) or recommended examination (WAEC, NECO, JAMB or Post-JAMB).
       * Once you resume tutorials in the morning, you are expected to submit your mobile device on the office desk before proceeding to the class or, do not come with the mobile device.
By any chance do you go into the class with the mobile device and get caught, you will be held responsible for the breach of agreement which attracts punishments and seizure of the device till further instructed to be released by the management.
